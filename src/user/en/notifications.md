# Notifications

Aipress24 keeps you informed of the activity that concerns you through two complementary channels: the **bell** (in-app notifications) and **email**. Most events trigger both.

## The bell

The **bell** icon in the header shows a badge with the number of unread notifications. Clicking it shows your most recent notifications (unread first), each with a short text and its timestamp.

- **Clicking** a notification marks it as read and takes you to the relevant page.
- **"Tout marquer comme lu"** (Mark all as read) sets all your notifications to "read".

## What triggers a notification

Here are the main events that notify you:

**Marketplace**

- An **application** is received on one of your offers.
- Your application was **selected** or **not retained**.

**Newsroom**

- A **topic** is pitched to you (editor-in-chief).
- Your topic was **accepted** or **rejected**.

**Enquiry notices**

- An **enquiry notice** is sent to you (or forwarded by a peer).
- A **meeting proposal** is made to you.
- An expert **accepted** or **declined** a meeting.

**Publications and purchases**

- You are **notified of a publication** in which you are quoted.
- A **proof of publication** concerns you.
- A member **gifts you an article** to read.
- You have acquired the **reproduction rights** to an article.

**Business Wall**

- You are invited to a **role** or a **partnership** on a Business Wall.
- A press-relations partnership has ended.
- You are invited to **join an organisation**.

## Bell and email

Most of these events reach you **both** in the bell and by email — a failure on one channel does not prevent the other. Important invitations and requests are thus delivered even when you are not logged in.
