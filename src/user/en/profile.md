# Your profile

![The account preferences](../../screenshots/preferences.png)

Your profile is your professional identity card on Aipress24. Its qualification (role, sectors, skills…) determines your visibility, your presence in the directory, and the relevance of matchmaking (enquiry notices, assignments, projects). A complete, up-to-date profile is therefore a major asset.

## Completing and editing your profile

Your profile information is collected during [registration](getting-started.md) (the KYC questionnaire) and can then be edited from **Preferences › "Modification du profil"** (Edit profile), which re-opens that same questionnaire.

The questionnaire is organised into sections: personal information, description of your organisation, matchmaking factors (roles, interests), hobbies and networking, and Business Wall.

!!! note "Changes subject to re-validation"
    Most changes are applied immediately. However, editing certain **sensitive fields** (surname, first name, title, main occupation) triggers a **re-validation** of your account by the team: your profile is temporarily put back on hold while it is reviewed.

## Visibility level

From **Preferences › "Visibilité du profil public"** (Public profile visibility), set how much detail other members can see:

- **Minimal information** ;
- **Sufficient information** (recommended level) ;
- **Detailed information**.

A live preview shows you what other members will see.

## Contact options

Some sensitive contact details — **login email**, **mobile phone** and **press-relations email** — can be selectively hidden.

From **Preferences › "Options de contact"** ("Who do you allow to see your contact details?"), you choose, for each of these three fields and for each **community** (journalists, communicators, experts…), who is allowed to see it. A member whose community is not authorised will see the value masked.

## Karma and reputation

Your name may be accompanied by a **karma medal** (gold, silver, bronze) reflecting your reputation on the platform. You can track your **reputation-performance index** in **Work › Performance**.

## Cover image and interests

- **Preferences › "Image de présentation"** — upload your profile cover banner (4:1 ratio), with a copyright notice.
- **Preferences › "Centres d'intérêts"** — enter your interests and hobbies (may be made public).

## Password and email address

- **Preferences › "Mot de passe"** — change your password.
- **Preferences › "Adresse email"** — change your login email; a confirmation email is sent to the new address to validate the change.
