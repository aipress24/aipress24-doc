# User guide

Welcome to Aipress24, the professional platform for the news and innovation ecosystem. This guide will help you get started and make the most of the platform, whether you are a **journalist**, a **communicator**, an **expert**, a **business leader**, or you represent an **organisation** (news outlet, press agency, PR agency, company, institution, association, academic body…).

!!! note
    The application's interface and features are currently focused on the French market, so most on-screen labels are in French. This guide gives the exact French labels in quotes for the actions you need to find, alongside their English explanation.

## Who Aipress24 is for

Aipress24 brings together five professional communities:

- **Press & Media** — newsrooms, journalists (with or without a press card), freelancers, news executives.
- **Communicators** — PR agencies, PR consultants, communication departments.
- **Leaders & Experts** — executives, experts, consultants, investors, event organisers, start-ups.
- **Transformers** — consulting and innovation players in organisational transformation.
- **Academics** — researchers, lecturers, PhD students, students, and higher-education institutions.

## The portals

Aipress24 is organised into several portals, available from the main navigation bar:

- **[News](news.md)** — the news feed from professional sources (articles and press releases).
- **[Social](social.md)** — your professional network: members, organisations and groups.
- **[Work](work.md)** — your workspace: editorial production, requests, sales.
- **[Marketplace](marketplace.md)** — assignments, projects, jobs, subscriptions and rights transfers.
- **[Events](events.md)** — the news agenda and accreditations.
- **[Search](search.md)** — cross-platform search (members, organisations, content).
