# The Social portal

**Social** is Aipress24's professional network. It lets you post, follow members and organisations, build up your contacts, and make your profile visible. It is organised around three spaces: the **Wall**, the **Members** directory and the **Organisations** directory.

## The Wall (activity feed)

The Wall is your social home page.

- **Post a message**: click in the "Écrivez un message…" (Write a message) field, type your text, then click **"Poster"** (Post). A confirmation is shown. (Messages are text only, with no length limit.)
- **Your feed** shows your own messages and those of the members you follow, newest first.
- Each message can be liked.

## The members directory

The directory lists every member of the platform, in alphabetical order. A counter shows the number of members matching your criteria.

- **Full-text search**: "Chercher (par nom, prénom, organisation, code postal, etc.)" — search by name, first name, organisation, postcode.
- **Filters** (combinable, shown as removable chips):
    - **Type Organisation**, **Type entreprise presse et média**, **Type presse & média**, **Types de PR Agencies**, **Tailles d'organisation** (organisation types and sizes) ;
    - **Secteur d'activité détaillés** (detailed sectors), **Compétences générales** / **Compétences journalisme** (general / journalism skills), **Transformations majeures** ;
    - **Fonction** (role) ;
    - **Pays**, **Département**, **Ville** (Country, Department, City).

These filters draw on the KYC qualification of profiles: the more complete your profile, the easier you are to find.

## A member's card

A member's card (opened by clicking their name) shows a cover image, their photo, their name (with a **karma** medal reflecting their reputation), their role and organisation, and a **"Suivre" / "Ne plus suivre"** (Follow / Unfollow) button.

It is organised into tabs:

- **Profil** — the qualified information (presentation, skills, languages, education, sectors, contact details…), displayed according to the visibility level chosen by the member.
- **Publications** — their published content.
- **Press Book** — the articles for which the member holds a proof of publication.
- **Activités** (Activities).
- **Groupes** (Groups) — their public groups.
- **Abonnements** (Following).
- **Abonnés** (Followers).

!!! note "Contact-detail privacy"
    Three fields (login email, mobile phone, press-relations email) can be masked. Their visibility depends on the **contact options** set by the profile owner: see [Your profile](profile.md).

## Organisations

The **Organisations** directory lists the organisations present on the platform. There are two kinds:

- An **"Auto" (unofficial) organisation**, created automatically from members' declarations.
- An **official organisation**, which has a **Business Wall** (subscription): it controls its page, its press contacts and its professional features.

An organisation's card shows its cover, logo, name, a **Follow** button, and tabs depending on its type: **À propos** (About), **Contacts**, **Publications**, **Press Book**, **Communiqués** (Press releases), **Événements** (Events).

If you are a member of an organisation that is still unofficial, a banner offers to "make your organisation's page official" — this is the activation of a [Business Wall](business-wall.md).

## Groups

- **Groups directory**: search and browse public groups.
- **Create a group**: the **+** button. Enter a **name** and a **description**, then create the group. You become its owner.
- **Join / Leave** a group from its card.

## Following members and organisations

The **Follow / Unfollow** button appears on every member and organisation card.

Following a source feeds your feed: their publications appear on your Wall and in the matching tabs of the [News](news.md) portal (**Agences**, **Médias**, **Journalistes**). Your lists appear under the **Following** and **Followers** tabs of your profile.
