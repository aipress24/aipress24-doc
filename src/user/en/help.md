# Help and support

## Getting help

- **FAQ** — check the frequently asked questions for the most common queries.
- **Contact** — if you can't find the answer to your question, write to us using the contact form.
- **Account and profile** — most settings (identity, contact details, notifications, password, visibility) are managed from **Social › "Paramétrer mon profil"** and the **Preferences** menu (see [Your profile](profile.md)).

## Frequently asked questions

**My account is not active yet.**
A new account is created *pending validation* and can only be used once approved by the Aipress24 team. See [Getting started](getting-started.md).

**How do I become visible to journalists?**
Complete your profile (sectors, skills, roles): this qualification feeds the directory, search, and enquiry-notice targeting. An organisation gains visibility by activating a [Business Wall](business-wall.md).

**Why can't I answer an enquiry notice?**
Answering an enquiry notice, on the organisation side, requires an **active Business Wall**. See [Enquiry notices](enquiry-notices.md).

**Who can register for an event?**
Event **accreditation is reserved for journalists**. See [Events](events.md).

**Where can I find my purchases, sales and invoices?**
In **Work › Achats** (Purchases), **Work › Ventes** (Sales) and **Work › Facturation** (Invoicing). See [Monetisation & rights](monetisation.md).

**How do I hide my phone number or email?**
From **Preferences › "Options de contact"**, community by community. See [Your profile](profile.md).

## Glossary

- **Business Wall (BW)** — an organisation's official showcase and its professional subscription.
- **BW Owner / Manager** — the executive responsible for a Business Wall / its administrator.
- **PR Manager (internal / external)** — a press-relations officer authorised to publish on behalf of an organisation (an employee, or a member of a partner agency).
- **Enquiry notice (avis d'enquête)** — a journalist's request to experts as part of an investigation.
- **Topic / Order (Sujet / Commande)** — a journalist's story pitch to a media outlet / an accepted, assigned topic.
- **Proof of publication (justificatif)** — official evidence that a piece has been published, usable in a Press Book.
- **Rights transfer (cession de droits)** — a licence for another outlet to reproduce a piece of content.
- **Consultation** — a paid reading right for an article, valid for one year.
- **Accreditation** — a journalist's registration for an event.
- **Karma** — a member's reputation index.
- **KYC** — the registration questionnaire that qualifies your profile.
- **Press Book** — a member's or organisation's collection of proofs of publication.
